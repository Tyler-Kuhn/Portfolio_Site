About me

I am a passionate front-end web developer dedicated to building responsive, user-friendly websites that merge functionality with minimalist design. My journey in tech began with a deep curiosity for how websites work, evolving into a committed pursuit of front-end development. I hold a certification in Software Testing from TripleTen, which has strengthened my attention to detail and commitment to delivering quality web experiences. When I’m not coding, you’ll find me rock climbing, embracing the challenges of the climb much like tackling complex coding problems—one step at a time. I’m excited about creating seamless digital experiences while continuously learning and growing in the ever-evolving world of web development.
